1. Information We Collect

  • • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • • Logistics Information: Delivery addresses, recipient names, and shipment details.
  • • Payment Information: Payment methods and transaction details.
  • • Communication Data: Customer inquiries, feedback, and communication history.
  • • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information.
  • • Tracking Information: Collected using TTELD tracking technology.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • We may collect the following types of personal information:
  • • Provide logistics and delivery services.
  • • Communicate with you about your shipments and inquiries.
  • • Process payments and manage transactions.
  • • Improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • • Send updates, promotional offers, and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • • Ensure the security and integrity of our systems.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We use the information we collect to:
