Policy

Last Updated: [03.10.2025]

GRAY CARGO LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or communicate with us.

1. Introduction

  • We are a logistics company that provides transportation and delivery services for goods and cargo. Our services include freight shipping, order tracking, and real-time delivery updates to ensure efficient and reliable transportation solutions for our customers. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information obtained through our website and services. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • We collect various types of personal information, including but not limited to:
  • • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • • Logistics Information: Delivery addresses, recipient names, and shipment details.
  • • Payment Information: Payment methods and transaction details.
  • • Communication Data: Customer inquiries, feedback, and communication history.
  • • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information.
  • • Tracking Information: Collected using TTELD tracking technology and cookies.

3. How We Collect Your Information

  • We gather personal information through the following methods:
  • • Directly from you when you fill out forms, place an order, or communicate with us.
  • • Automatically via cookies, tracking technologies, and third-party analytics.
  • • From third-party service providers that help facilitate payments and deliveries.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • We use your personal information to:
  • • Provide logistics and delivery services.
  • • Communicate with you about your shipments and inquiries.
  • • Process payments and manage transactions.
  • • Improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • • Send updates, promotional offers, and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • • Ensure the security and integrity of our systems.

5. Sharing Your Information

  • We may share your information with:
  • • Service Providers: Third-party companies that assist us in providing our services (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners).
  • • Business Partners: When necessary for joint marketing activities or promotions.
  • • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • • Third-Party Tracking Tools: Information collected via TTELD for tracking purposes.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. SMS Consent and Communication

  • By opting in to receive SMS messages from GRAY CARGO LLC, you agree to receive messages related to:
  • • Shipment updates and delivery notifications.
  • • Appointment reminders and confirmations.
  • • Marketing promotions and offers (with your consent).
  • Your Rights:
  • • Messaging frequency may vary.
  • • Message and data rates may apply.
  • • You can opt out at any time by texting STOP.
  • • For assistance, text HELP or contact us at info@graycargo.com or call 706-622-5656.
  • SMS opt-in or phone numbers for the SMS are not shared with any third party or affiliate company for marketing purposes.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • We use TTELD and other tracking technologies to:
  • • Monitor website performance and user interactions.
  • • Enhance user experience and optimize website functionality.
  • • Analyze traffic and marketing effectiveness.

8. Data Security and Retention:

  • We implement security measures, including encryption, access controls, and secure storage, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We retain your personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.
